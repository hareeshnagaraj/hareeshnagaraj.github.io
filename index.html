About Me

I consider myself a composer - the process of creation, making something from nothing, is something that has always fascinated me. While learning to play guitar when I was younger, I always found myself writing my own songs more than anything else. Learning to code, I found the same appeal. To me, the similarities are boundless and the intersection of logic and creativity is where I like to spend my time. tabdat

tabdat is intended to help you find tabs easier - instead of searching through multiple websites painstakingly, tabdat allows you to find tabs from the best sites with a swipe of your finger. Upon installation, tabdat generates a catalog of the music currently on your phone, making it simple to search tabs for songs already on your device. In addition, tabdat supports song recognition a la Shazam, to allow you to find tabs on the fly. You can also search and store tabs from the internet for any artist.

mapme

mapme is an npm module to help visualize an application's dependency structure with an easy to understand tree visualization. Simply run map me on the starting point of your app(eg app.js or server.js) and mapme will open an interactive d3 based tree showing you what your file dependencies are for your project.

View On NPM

boysenberry

boysenberry is a lightweight version of the developer tools to check out the layout of a page, and inspect the relevant CSS without having to open your developer tools. boysenberry also adds shortcuts to copy style of an element, displays layout bounds of all page elements, and lets you switch between basic and computed style with a few simple shortcuts.
